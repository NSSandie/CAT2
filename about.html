Our Story

Built by Students, for Students

Student Hub was founded in 2024 by a group of BBIT students at Nairobi's leading university who believed that academic information should be easy to find, beautifully presented, and always accessible.

Today, the platform serves over 4,200 students across multiple faculties — connecting learners with courses, events, faculty profiles, and digital resources in one seamless experience.
